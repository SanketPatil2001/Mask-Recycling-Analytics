How municipal plays an important role?

A Municipality is a administrative division composed of a defined territory and population. Waste Management is the collection, transport, processing, recycling or disposal and monitoring the waste materials. Methods of collection of waste are either door-to-door or using containers or communal bins. As per our project, an extra bin is kept in a residential area for the collection of used masks which will be further transported for recycling. Members of the society or a particular locality will deposit their masks in the bin and the garbage collecting trucks throughout the city would collect it and take it to the dumping yard. From their the collected mask bins will be sent to the receiver company. This helps to manage the bio-medical waste and keep our surrounding clean.
